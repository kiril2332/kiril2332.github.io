About me

Hello! My name is Kiril, and I am 20 years old. I come from Skopje, Macedonia. My educational path began at OU Grigor Prlicev and continued at SUGS Rade Jovcesvski Korcagin when programming was first introduced to me. Now, I am taking my bachelor's degree in software engineering at the Faculty of Computer Science and Education in Skopje.

My time at FCSE isn't just about gaining a degree; it's about shaping the future and how I would contribute to the tech world with relevant, impacting solutions. When I look forward, I see myself being an innovator and problem-solver. It thrills me that what lies ahead is full of opportunities and new experiences, from crafting innovative applications to offering up my resources in service of projects that will impact the global community.
